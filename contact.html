FAQ

how to cancel booking?

To cancel your booking, log in to your account,and get in touch and send message about your booking that you want to cancel.For further assistance, contact our support team.

when will I get the possession?

Ready-to-move-in properties: Immediate possession after payment and paperwork. Under-construction properties: As per the developer’s timeline mentioned in the agreement

where can I pay the rent?

Currently, rent payments are processed offline. Please contact your property manager or our support team for payment instructions and available payment methods.

how to contact with the buyers?

To contact buyers, you can reach out through the contact information provided on their property inquiry. If you're a registered, you can use the messaging feature on our website.

why my listing not showing up?

Your listing may not be showing up due to incomplete details, or if it has expired or been marked inactive.It’s also possible that there could be temporary technical issues on the website.

how to promote my listing?

Sharing your listing on social media or through your network can also help increase its exposure. If you need further guidance, feel free to contact our support team.
